Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Geesly (“we,” “our,” “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our dating app available on Android and iOS.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number, date of birth, gender, photos)
  • Location data (for match suggestions and features)
  • Login information via Google, Apple, or Facebook
  • Payment and subscription data (via Google Play, Apple, and RevenueCat)
  • Analytics and ad data to improve app performance

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To suggest and match compatible users
  • To process payments and subscriptions
  • To improve the app’s safety, performance, and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We may share information with trusted third parties, including Firebase, Google, Apple, Facebook, and RevenueCat, for authentication, analytics, and payment processing. We never sell your personal data.

4. Location Tracking

Geesly requires location access to show nearby matches. You can disable location services in your device settings, but certain features may not function properly.

5. Security

We use Firebase Security Rules, encryption, and other measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account information at any time through the app. To request data deletion, contact us at geeslyhelp@gmail.com.

7. Advertising and Analytics

We may use tools like Google Analytics or AdMob to understand usage patterns and display relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with the new effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at geeslyhelp@gmail.com.
