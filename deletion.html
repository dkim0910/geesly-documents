Account Deletion

Last updated: November 2025

Request Account Deletion

To request the permanent deletion of your Geesly account and all associated data, please send an email to our support team.

Please include the following information in your email to help us process your request:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Your Geesly Public ID (if you know it)
  • The email address or phone number used to create your account

Send your request to:

geeslyhelp@gmail.com


Our team will review your request and contact you to confirm the deletion. Once confirmed, this action is permanent and cannot be reversed. Your profile, matches, messages, and all other personal data will be erased from our systems in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
